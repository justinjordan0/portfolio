About Project

Appomart has developed an IT project that presents a formidable challenge: Toolly, a B2C outsourcing platform that enables customers to quickly and easily place orders for a wide range of household, professional, and educational services, and allows companies and specialists to conveniently and regularly receive orders. This application implements functions for creating orders for a variety of categories, as well as for setting additional unique parameters.

Key

Android, iOS, Kotlin, Swift, Firebase Auth, Firestore Database, Messaging, Google Maps, RESTful API
