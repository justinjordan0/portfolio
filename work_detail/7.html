About Project

We have developed perfect Farm goods Online Shop using our own e-commerce engine for iOS and Android. Tasty Farm combines the functionality of online shop and a catalog of recipes for cooking from farm products in the convinient stories format.

One can create own account by SMS, specifies your delivery addresses, makes a delivery order for another person. There is a vast product catalog, and function of shopping cart may allows you proceed to the procedure of ordering and delivery to a selected address.

Key

Android, iOS, Kotlin, Swift, Firebase, Payment API
