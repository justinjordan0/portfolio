About Project

Designed by a dentist! Timer app!
The app that will teach you how to brush your teeth properly in 2.5 minutes.
No special devices: only a toothpaste and a toothbrush.
For all ages: the video is useful for both adults and children. The child will especially enjoy repeating the movements from the cartoon.
